Great Sphinx of Giza

The second most important tourist place in Giza

The Great Sphinx of Giza is a limestone statue of a reclining sphinx, a mythical creature with the head of a human, andthe body of a lion.[1] Facing directly from west to east, it stands on the Giza Plateau on the west bank of the Nile in Giza,Egypt. The face of the Sphinx appears to represent the pharaoh Khafre.[2]
